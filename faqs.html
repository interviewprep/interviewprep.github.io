FAQ'S

1. What is the duration of the boot camp?

It is divided into two parts, Basic and Advanced. Each part is held over a weekend (both Saturday and Sunday).

2. What is the cost of the boot camp?

The Basic and Advanced workshop each cost $399. Even if the bootcamp decreases your interview preparation time by just a week, it more than pays for itself.

Many of our past attendees have been able to get the boot camp fee tax exempted, typically decreasing the cost by a third or even get the cost reimbursed in full by their employer. Drop us an email to know more.

3. Can I just get a mock interview?

Yes, absolutely! Mock interviews are conducted over the phone and will closely resemble that of top tech companies.

Mock interviews cost $75.

4. Is this for me if I am not looking for companies like Google and Facebook?

In most companies your interview performance will determine your salary, grade and negotiating power.

5. What will be the focus of the boot camp?

The boot camp is a gruelling course to hone your programming interview skills in the most economic and time efficient way possible.

Our primary focus will be brushing up technical concepts on core programming interview topics including recursion, dynamic programming and design questions, identifying problem solving patterns that will help you quickly formulate a solution, and developing skills to write error free code despite interview pressure.

We will also touch upon behavioral questions and offer negotiation strategies exclusive to software engineering interviews.

6. What if I don’t like the boot camp?

We don’t want your money if we can’t help you. Despite the incredibly low cost, we are happy to refund you the entire cost of the workshop if we were not able to help you in spite of our and your best efforts. Drop us an email to discuss the terms and conditions.

7. Will you help us after the boot-camp?

Of course! We are here to help whether you ran into a tough interview question or need help negotiating an offer.
